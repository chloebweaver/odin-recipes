Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a pasta dish traditionally made with guanciale, eggs, and cheese, but this will be a vegetarian version.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (of your choice; I prefer linguine, personally)
  • Cremini mushrooms
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino or Parmesan
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Begin by filling a large pot with water and salt. Make the water as salty as the Mediteranean Sea
  2. Toss the mushrooms with salt.
  3. While the water boils, in a large pan, add the butter and the diced mushrooms.
  4. When the mushrooms are well cooked, add the diced garlic.
  5. Add the linguine (whole! not broken) to the pot and cook.
  6. Now to begin the sauce: Separate the yolk out from one egg, and then add one whole egg.
  7. Add the cheese to the eggs, mixing thoroughly. Add a crack of pepper.
  8. Setting aside a mug of pasta water, drain the pasta.
  9. Now, mix together the mushrooms, pasta, and sauce, using the pasta water to maintain a good consistency.
  10. Serve.
