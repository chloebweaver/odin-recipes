Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a pasta dish with tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed red pepper
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Basil (dry or fresh)
  • White wine
  • Burrata
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and begin boiling. Once water is boiling, add spaghetti.
  2. Meanwhile, dice the onion and begin cooking in a large pan with the olive oil.
  3. Once onion is translucent, add garlic and crushed red pepper and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add petit diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, salt, sugar, white wine, and basil. Cook until sauce is thick.
  5. Once the pasta is cooked, set aside some of the water and drain.
  6. Mix pasta with sauce, using the set-aside water liberally and sprinkling in the parmesan.
  7. Plate the pasta and add three or so tears of burrata to each plate.
  8. Serve.
