Gnocchi

Description

Gnocchi is a kind of potato dumpling. Presented here is a relatively simple gnocchi recipe.

Ingredients

  • Gnocchi
  • Butter
  • Sage
  • Salt
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil the gnocchi in pot with water (salted).
  2. Meanwhile, warm the butter, sage, and salt over medium heat. Once the butter turns golden brown, remove from heat.
  3. Toss the butter with the gnocchi and sprinkle on parmesan.
  4. Serve.
